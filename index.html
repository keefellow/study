My First Heading

You are invited to participate in a research study on how practice of mindful affirmation via a mobile app may have an effect on your well-being during this COVID-19 pandemic. This study is conducted by Assistant Professor Kee Ying Hwa, National Institute of Education, Singapore from Nanyang Technological University. This study will be conducted over 8 days, as you use the app to record your practice of mindful affirmations counts. You will first be asked to decide on a phrase that you believe is personally meaningful which you can use as an affirmation. In this study, you will be asked to repeat this phrase as often as possible, while using the app to You will be asked to complete two online surveys on Day 1 and Day 8. For the first 10 days, you will be asked to play the allocated icon tapping game designed to help you develop the ability to refocus at least once a day. Within the app, you will also find some relevant mental skills related tips to help you do your best in the task. You will get a record of your performance trials as you progress. There is also an option in the app for you to communicate with the researcher, should you have any query or needed help with understanding the tips. A total of 120 participants, who are athletes, will be recruited for this research. Your participation in this study is completely voluntary, and you have the right to withdraw from the study at any time without any penalty. You may skip also any questions you do not wish to answer. If you do not wish to complete the study, simply delete the mobile application. Your participation in this research will be kept confidential.
